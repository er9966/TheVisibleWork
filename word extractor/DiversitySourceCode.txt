<h2>Explore diversity</h2>
<div class="row">
<div class="col-md-11">
<p class="intro">At the University of Washington, diversity is integral to excellence. We value and honor diverse experiences and perspectives, strive to create welcoming and respectful learning environments, and promote access, opportunity and justice for all.</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="box-outer">
<div class="box three box-none">
<div class="tile">
<div><a href="https://www.washington.edu/diversity/learning-about-difference/"><img class="size-half wp-image-2998 alignnone" src="https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/uw-s3-cdn/wp-content/uploads/sites/48/2016/01/06195851/GO-MAP_2-375x250.jpg" alt="GO-MAP_2" width="375" height="250" /></a></div>
<h3><a title="Creating New Knowledge, Learning About Difference" href="http://www.washington.edu/diversity/learning-about-difference/">Learning about difference</a></h3>
<p>The study of diversity fosters an understanding of cultural traditions, histories and influences.</p>
</div>
<div class="tile">
<div><a href="https://www.washington.edu/diversity/makingchange/"><img class="alignnone wp-image-3101 size-half" src="https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/uw-s3-cdn/wp-content/uploads/sites/48/2016/01/06201030/Mochi-Lab-375x250.jpg" alt="Mochi-Lab" width="375" height="250" /></a></div>
<h3><a title="Making Change, Transforming Lives" href="http://www.washington.edu/diversity/makingchange/">Making change, transforming lives</a></h3>
<p>Students, faculty and staff strive to improve the well-being of our communities.</p>
</div>
<div class="tile">
<div><a href="http://www.washington.edu/diversity/world-changing-research/"><img class="alignnone wp-image-3103 size-half" src="https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/uw-s3-cdn/wp-content/uploads/sites/48/2016/01/06201144/angelina-godoy-375x250.jpg" alt="Angelina Godoy" width="375" height="250" /></a></div>
<h3><a href="http://www.washington.edu/diversity/world-changing-research/">World changing research</a></h3>
<p>UW faculty conduct research on the benefits of cultural diversity, as well as its challenges.</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>

<h3>Leadership</h3>
<p>Vice President for Minority Affairs &amp; Diversity Rickey Hall is the <strong><a href="http://www.washington.edu/diversity/cdo/">university diversity officer</a></strong> at the University of Washington. Hall has oversight of the UW’s progress in diversity, as well as programs administered by the <strong><a href="http://www.uw.edu/omad/">Office of Minority Affairs &amp; Diversity</a></strong>.</p>
